    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Didn't find your question answered? Write our support team.

    How do I change my password?
    Click in settings > security > password > change password, insert new and old password and confirm
    How can I withdraw my funds?
    Go to Bank > withdraw > chose the currency and the amount to withdraw and confirm, insert your PIN and confirm again
    How do I know if I leveled up?
    All level related requirements and achievements you can check in the Status tab
    How can I change my email?
    It's not possible for you to change your email.
    Where can I check my earnings?
    You can check in dashboard or in the bank tab
    How do i contact support?
    Go to Support > create a ticket, fill all informations and click Send, someone of our team will be in touch with you in 24-72 hours
    How can I claim my prize?
    Upon leveling up/meeting the prize criteria, you are going to receive an email with the instructions on how to claim it, if you reached a level/criteria and did not received an email please contact support
    Where can I download the company official marketing materials?
    Go to Promotions tab, at the bottom you are going to see all the available materials
    I just referred a friend, how do I know if he registered?
    Go to Unilevel tab, there you will see all referrals from your first to the fifth level
    How can I see my binary tree points?
    Go to Binary Tree Tab, there you can check all points from your team in all currencies by clicking in the left side on the currency you wish to check on
    How can I refer a friend?
    Copy your link and share with your friends, when they register they will appear in your Unilevel Tab
    How to activate 2-Factor authentication?
    Go to settings > Security > 2-Authentication Factor and follow the instructions
    How do I contact my referrer/sponsor?
    Go to Settings > General Settings there you will be able to see your sponsor's name and if he any social media you will be able to contact him/her
    How to activate my other binary matrixes?
    In order to activate a matrix you must have an active deposit on that currency, you can do that going in Bank> Deposit choose the currency you would like to deposit in, choose the amount and confirm copy the address and send the exact amount to the wallet informed
    I just made a withdrawal, how long to be in my wallet?
    Amounts lesser then 100 dollars are made automatically amounts greater than that take up to 24 hours to be in your wallet
    Has being more than 24 hours and I still did not received my withdrawal, what should I do?
    Please contact support immediately and report the problem
    I found a glitch/bug what should I do?
    Please contact support
    How can I raise my invested amount?
    Go to Bank > Deposit choose the currency and amount and click Confirm, copy the address and send the exact amount to the address informed
    Where can I find the tutorials?
    Go to Promotion Tab, there you will see a specific area for video tutorials
    I created a deposit request in the wrong currency, what do I do now?
    Just ignore the wrong request and create a new one in the correct currency. Requests automatically cancel after 5 hours
    I made a deposit and has being more than 5 hours and yet the amount didn't appeared in my dashboard?
    Try to refresh page and clear cache, if after that the amount did not appeared please contact Support immediately with the transaction hash and print of the amount sent from your wallet
